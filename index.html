AR and VR for Community Engagement

Discover how AR and VR can be used to transform community engagement in planning and design.

Introduction to the Project

This website is part of the research outputs for a PhD looking at how Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR) can support community engagement in the planning of built environments.

Using a participatory approach with stakeholders in place-making and planning, the research focuses on how these immersive technologies can foster trust when used as engagement tools. The goal is to develop ideas on delivery methods that promote trust and transparency.

Introduction to the Website

This website is a resource for communities, researchers, planners, designers, and developers interested in using immersive technologies for community engagement in planning. Explore various AR and VR examples, discover engagement delivery models developed through research, and learn about content creation methods that were also part of the research.

Delivery Models

Through the research a number of alternative delivery models where designed that aim to foster trust when VR and AR are used for engagement during community planning.

Ways to Create Content

Through the research a number of alternative ways of creating content were devleoped.

The Content Itself

The content generated through AR and VR technologies ranges from simple 3D models to immersive storytelling environments.
